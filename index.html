How Does School Fee
Financing Work?

Many schools today ask parents to pay the fees for the whole year upfront.
Sometimes, parents do not have the necessary funds, so they struggle to make
the payment. When you opt for education loan finance plans, your preferred
financier will pay the required amount to the school. You can then pay them
affordable EMIs. The idea is to take the burden of a large payment off parents
while ensuring their child gets the education they deserve.
